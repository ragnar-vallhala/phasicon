Remote health metric monitoring and disease prediction system containing two parts, a rechargable wearable device in the form of a small patch that sticks on the upper pectoralis muscle whose job is to relay essential data such as heart rate/ body temperature/ sleep analysis/stress response/ blood oxygenation/ fall and activity detection, and a sensor that is plugged into a socket in a room to create a 3d construction of the environment and tracks a person’s movement/ posture/activity/movement patterns data in real time.
